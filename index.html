Ecological resilience is enhanced, as scientifically detected trends can be complemented with observed local changes. Through the use of a multidimensional knowledge base, MPA effectiveness is improved, long-term adaptability of governance is supported, and greater social and ecological desirability is achieved for the protected area’s governance system. Both governance vitality and the overall effectiveness and efficiency of protection are increased because governance and management are positioned within the wider ecological, political, and social system that influences them. Governance vitality and adaptive governance

S1.1 Enable and ensure governance vitality at all levels, including governance by government, by private individuals and organizations, by Indigenous Peoples and/or local communities as well as shared governance by various rightsholders and stakeholders together (Borrini-Feyerabend et al. 2013).
